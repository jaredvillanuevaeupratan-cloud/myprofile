Our Story

Baking Excellence Since 1985

Nestled in the heart of the city, Lacoste Bakery has been serving the community with freshly baked goods for over three decades. Our founder, Jean-Pierre Lacoste, brought traditional French baking techniques from Paris, combining them with locally sourced ingredients to create something truly special.


Every morning at 4 AM, our skilled bakers begin their craft, using time-honored recipes and modern innovation to bring you the finest breads, pastries, and desserts. We believe in quality over quantity, which is why everything is made fresh daily in small batches.


Our commitment to sustainability means we work with local farmers and use eco-friendly packaging. When you choose Lacoste Bakery, you're not just getting exceptional food—you're supporting a community-focused business that cares.
